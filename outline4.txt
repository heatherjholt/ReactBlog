REFLECTION 

What changed between your Stage 1 outline and your final implementation? Why did you change it?
I first used a plain 404 page message. I changed it to use <Link> so users could get back to the home page if they hit a broken URL.

What was the hardest part to implement correctly, and why?
The hardest part was managing the global state. Because both the Blog pageand the New Post page needed access to the blog posts. Using `setPosts` function and using `.filter()` to delete posts without changing the original array took some work.

Describe one mistake you made and how you fixed it.
The delete button was not initially working. I did not have handleDelete() assigned correctly for the button function. I also forgot to add validation for empty boxes at first.
